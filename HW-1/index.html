Information About Bridget

Bridget Unterreiner is currently a Senior at the University of Montana pursuing a fully online Bachelor of Arts in Media Arts. Residing in Kalispell, MT, she also currently owns and operates Blumen Haus, a freelance floral design business that brings artful and unique floral creations to clients in the Flathead Valley. Prior to University of Montana and the creation of Blumen Haus, Bridget received her Associate of Arts from Flathead Valley Community College in Kalispell. Through FVCC, Bridget had the opportunity to live and study abroad in Venice, Italy in the Spring of 2016. Prior to FVCC, she obtained her Associate of Applied Science in Advertising and Marketing Communications from the Fashion Institute of Technology in New York City. Bridget was born in Pasadena, California but raised in Kalispell, Montana. She grew up with an intense passion for tap dance. She enjoys playing with flowers, listening to music, and hanging with friends.

My Business

In the spring of 2017, Bridget started Blumen Haus, a freelance floral design business that operates out of a home studio and provides floral services to clients who are looking for unique arrangements for their homes, offices, special events, etc. Blumen Haus was born out of a passion for all things floral and the desire to create!
